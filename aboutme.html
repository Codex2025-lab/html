About Me

Fredrick Robinson studies for a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology at the University of Belize in Belmopan. He completed an MBA at the National Tsing Hua University in Taiwan, and his undergraduate degree is in Computer Science. He teaches Management Information Systems courses to business students as well as Fundamentals of Computing and Database courses at the University of Belize. His current research focuses on information system success from a developing country perspective. He is also active in initiatives aimed at making programming classes available at all secondary schools in Belize.
